PayTheory Billing Integration

Product Case Study ‚Ä¢ Teligant Platform

The Problem: Inflexible Business Model

Initial platform design only supported monthly subscriptions, but user research revealed different patient preferences: some wanted annual plans for savings, others needed quarterly for cash flow. We needed billing flexibility to support diverse go-to-market strategies.

Product Lifecycle

Phase 1: Business Requirements Discovery (Ideation)

Through stakeholder interviews and patient research, identified key requirements:

  • Annual memberships for price-conscious patients ($499/year vs. $49/month)
  • Quarterly options for mid-range commitment
  • Multi-brand support with different billing strategies per brand
  • Flexible membership benefits tied to subscription type
  • Easy subscription modification for patients

Business Requirement

Support multiple subscription models from day one to enable diverse go-to-market strategies across multi-tenant platform. Each brand should be able to configure its own billing options.

Phase 2: Vendor Evaluation & Architecture (Validation)

Evaluated payment processors based on:

  • Flexible subscription cycle support (30-365 days)
  • HIPAA compliance and BAA availability
  • API quality and webhook reliability
  • Healthcare-specific features
  • Pricing structure for healthcare SaaS

Selected PayTheory for superior subscription flexibility and healthcare focus.

Phase 3: Technical Design & Specifications (Implementation)

Designed flexible subscription architecture supporting 30-365 day billing cycles:

Architecture Components

  • <strong>Subscription Plans:</strong> Configurable billing cycles at brand level
  • <strong>Membership Benefits:</strong> Per-plan benefit configuration (e.g., annual members get extra consultation credits)
  • <strong>Payment Methods:</strong> Credit card storage with PCI compliance
  • <strong>Billing Engine:</strong> Automated charging, retry logic, dunning management
  • <strong>Webhooks:</strong> Real-time payment status updates
  • <strong>Admin Tools:</strong> Subscription management, refunds, adjustments

Wrote detailed integration specs for PayTheory implementation:

  • Subscription creation with custom billing cycles
  • Subscription modification (upgrade/downgrade/pause)
  • Cancellation with prorated refunds
  • Payment retry logic for failed charges
  • Webhook handling for payment events
  • Admin interface for subscription management

Phase 4: Development & Integration

Implementation across full stack:

  • Backend: PayTheory API integration with 8 endpoints
  • Database: Subscription models with flexible cycle support
  • Frontend: Subscription selection UI with clear pricing
  • Admin: Brand-level billing configuration
  • Webhooks: Real-time payment status processing
  • Error handling: Payment failures, retry logic, customer notifications

Phase 5: Testing & Validation

Comprehensive testing across billing scenarios:

  • Subscription creation for monthly, quarterly, annual plans
  • Payment success and failure scenarios
  • Retry logic and dunning workflows
  • Subscription modifications and cancellations
  • Prorated refunds and adjustments
  • Multi-brand configuration testing

Phase 6: Launch & Business Model Enablement

Launched with multiple subscription options from day one:

  • Hedfirst: $499/year AI Care Membership as primary model
  • Alternative: $49/month for lower commitment
  • Future brands: Flexibility to test different pricing strategies
  • A/B testing capability for pricing optimization
